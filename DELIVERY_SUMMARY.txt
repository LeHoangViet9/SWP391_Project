🔐 THYMELEAF FORM BINDING:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

All forms include proper Thymeleaf bindings:
• th:action="@{/api/v1/endpoint}" - Form action
• th:object="${formObject}" - Form object
• th:field="*{fieldName}" - Field binding
• th:value="${value}" - Field values
• th:each="item : ${list}" - Iteration
• th:if="${condition}" - Conditional
• th:classappend="${condition} ? 'class1' : 'class2'" - Dynamic styling
